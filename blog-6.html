10 reasons why American Veterinary Clinic is your only vet of choice.

Why Choose American Veterinary Clinic?

We understand that you have several options when choosing the best veterinary clinic hospital in UAE for your pet’s veterinary care. Thank you for considering the American Veterinary Clinic for that care. While there are other pet veterinary specialist hospitals in UAE, we believe these 10 reasons set us apart:

Expertise

We have experienced veterinarians in the UAE, bringing together a team that is highly trained to offer comprehensive vet care to your pets. We are all very passionate about what we are doing and promise to treat them like one of our own. Our expertise ensures that your pet receives the highest quality medical attention whenever they meet with us.

Comprehensive Services

As the best veterinary specialist hospital in UAE, we offer a variety of services to cater to all your pet’s needs. Choosing us will benefit you with multi-doctor practice including routine check-ups, complex surgical procedures, diagnostic testing, and emergency care. This approach enables us to consult even in serious cases within our practice. Hence, we will help you facilitate the best decision for your pet’s well-being.

State-of-the-Art Equipment

To provide superior care to your furry friend, we utilize the latest technology for accurate diagnosis and treatment. In our pet veterinary specialist hospital in UAE, we make significant investments in state-of-the-art medical equipment. We are equipped with advanced imaging, laboratory, surgical, and therapeutic lasers, and a whole lot more.

Preventive Care

We believe in the adage that “prevention is better than cure”, and that’s the reason why we are dedicated to preventive care. Our preventive care includes a vaccination program where you can reserve the day conveniently to keep your pets in the pink of health. We stand by our motto and help your furry companion from any potential health issues before they arise.

Emergency Care

When you have a life-threatening emergency for your pet, you can trust the American Veterinary Clinic. We are well-equipped and capable of handling all emergencies, ensuring that your pet receives urgent medical attention when it’s needed the most.

Clean and Safe Environment

Our experienced veterinarians know the importance of a clean environment, which is crucial for the well-being of pets. We maintain clean and safe facilities, from our landscaping to our surgical suites, we promote hygiene. Our commitment to a clean environment ensures that both the pets and their owners feel comfortable during their visit to our clinic.

Client Education

If you’re a pet enthusiast or beginner, you should understand the key responsibilities of pet ownership. Our experienced veterinary consultants educate pet owners about the various aspects of pet care including nutrition and behavior. We are happy to empower pet owners with superior and appropriate knowledge of pet care.

Personalized Treatment

Every pet is unique, so their medical needs may vary. It is important to diagnose pets based on their age and breed. The American Veterinary Clinic is a master in providing personalized treatment, and we understand the need for tailored treatment plans. This personalized approach ensures that your pet receives the best care based on individual requirements.

Compassion and Care

We consider pets to be a beloved member of the family. Our entire staff wants to give what is best for your pet, that is the reason why we prioritize physical and emotional well-being for both you and your pets.

Availability

We understand that pet emergencies can happen at any time. That’s why we are open Monday through Friday from 07.30 AM to 10.00 PM and Saturday, Sunday, or any other public holiday from 08.00 AM to 05.00 PM. You can also connect with us through our after-hour emergency care services.
