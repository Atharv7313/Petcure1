About Us

PETCURE is a trusted veterinary clinic based in Abu Dhabi with over 25 years of experience in providing compassionate, high-quality care for pets. Originally known as American Veterinary Clinic, PETCURE rebranded to reflect its modern approach and commitment to offering innovative, personalized veterinary solutions.
